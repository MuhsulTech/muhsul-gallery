How to pay for my order? To pay for the selected item click on buy button in each item you wish to buy it will take you to our payment site in paystack, fill out the form and send us with the payment receipt with the item ID Number via whatsapp for varification.

How to collect my order after payment? To collect your ordered items from us you are to include your address during payment process so that when your order is shipped you will be notified, but you are to give 500 Naira to the person delivered your order to you, Unless when you bought many items so that you can enjoy free delivery charges.

How long should I wait for my ordered items? Maximum order time is two weeks, but some times your order can be available before two weeks depending on the shipping time but your order cannot exceed more than two weeks.
